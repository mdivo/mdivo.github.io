ParallelTracks is a platform that connects music lovers with emerging and undiscovered artists who share similar musical identities, styles and sounds as their mainstream counterparts. ParallelTracks provides a place where users can discover and support hidden gems in the vast music industry.

The projects aim to create a user-friendly, interactive directory for comparisons between small and mainstream artist by implementing Bootstrap for the frontend styling, and HTML/JavaScript/CSS for more styling and for frontend functionalities to communicated with the backend. The project will be developed using Django and Python and as the standard way of music recommendation from streaming services suggests songs that someone similar music taste enjoys. View website

Took part in a UX design workshop at BrainStation London. I was able to learn more about design thinking and the process of how a UX design goes from conception to production and learn industry insights from industry experts. We were tasked at using design thinking concepts to create a simple UX interface for a 'fresh market produce delivery app'.

Started with asking market-goers questions and figuring out problems to then sketching the interface in 5 minutes. The sketches where imported into Marvel App on our phones the create a very quick wireframe from the sketches. We were then tasked to re-create the wireframe on Figma in just 30 minutes. Due to short time to create the wireframe, there are many components missing however, my design was votes 2nd Best Design at the workshop, for its welcoming interface and detail in wireframing.
